robust portfolio optimization


θ^ 估计的不准确常常是导致portfolio optimization模型失效的主因。

robust optimization三种方式
stochastic robust optimization(只考虑平均情况)
worst-case robust optimization（只考虑最坏情况）
chance robust optimization.（折中）


robust portfolio optimization conquer against
estimation errors of the parameters μ and Σ.


robust maximum return optimization :with mean under elliptical uncertainty, from LP to SOCP

robust minimum variance optimization:with covariance under convex uncertainty set, from QP to SOCP
robust markowitz optimization: with mean and covariance under box or ellipse uncertainty, model will both become SDP

特例：Variance Uncertainty based on Factor Model

robust sharp ratio optimization:model will both become SDP
